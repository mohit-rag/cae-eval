Scapy's logging is inconsistent and too noisy. Packet dissection messages like "DNS RR prematured end" or "bad dataofs" show up as warnings even though they are just informational notes about malformed input, not real problems. This causes users to suppress all logging entirely and then miss actual issues. On top of that, several deprecated functions announce themselves through the logger instead of using Python's standard warnings module, the console log handler is not configured until interact() is called so scripted usage gets no output at all, and various networking and architecture modules use the wrong logger for their context.
